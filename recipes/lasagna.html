Brenda's Lasanga

Description

This delicious dish recipe is brought to you by Brenda, who is one of the best chef in the world. She has won the master chef Australia 2020 as well.

Nutritional values per serving

643 calories; protein 41.3g; carbohydrates 53.4g; fat 29.3g; cholesterol 108.3mg; sodium 707.3mg

Ingredents

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
